Welcome to the WWI face database!

This database is meant for researchers to upload photos and find a potential face match for a previously unidentified person. Please see the copyright page for more information or if you have questions.

To upload a photo, ensure that the name of the photo is the name of the person in it. Ensure that there are no extra characters in the title.
